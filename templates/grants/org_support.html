Applying for a grant

Eligibility
Please review our eligibility criteria before applying for a grant.
Application process
1. Register for an account
All this requires is an email, password, and your organization's name. Choose an email that you expect to have access to in future years.
2. Fill out the online form
  • The bottom of your home page has a links of SJF grant cycles. Click the link on an open cycle to begin filling out the form.
  • Upload your budget form, grant funding sources and diversity chart.
  • Progress on the application is automatically saved every 30 seconds. You can close the window and find it later under "saved drafts" on your home page.

Accounts

Lost password
If you've forgotten your password, go here to reset it. You'll need to enter the email that was used to register your organization and a link will be sent to that address.
Forgotten email/login
If you forgot or no longer have access to the email used to register your organization, please contact us to have the email on your account updated.
